# Three-Team KPI Calculation Guide

This file is retained for backward compatibility.

The live project now supports nine teams, including Submission, and uses the unified KPI scoring model everywhere:

- KPI Achievement may exceed 100%.
- Effective Achievement = `min(KPI Achievement, 100)`.
- KPI Contribution is capped by KPI Weight.
- Final Performance Score is the sum of all KPI contributions and cannot exceed 100%.
